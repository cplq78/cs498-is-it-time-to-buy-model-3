Full Self-Driving(FSD) Price

Full Self-Driving Analysis

Another selling point in Tesla Model 3s is the Full Self-Driving (FSD) capability. At an article published by Electrek, they stated that "Elon Musk evaluated the complete version of the FSD package at over $100,000." As we can note on the graph the price, the price has seen some fluctuation. However, the price has gotten to 10 percent of Musk's evaluation. The lowest cost for the feature was $6,000, from early April/2019 to mid August/2019.
Currently, the FSD feature is priced at $8,000.
