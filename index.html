Tesla Model 3 Long Range Dual Motor Price History

Vehicle Price Analysis

As we can see from the graph price history below, this particular model configuration has seen some variability with its price. With vehicles being delivered starting late July/18, we can see that those vehicles came with a price of $54,000 without adding any ‘add ons,’ such as upgraded tires, or colors. Starting early September/2018, the price went up by $1,000 for a total of $55,000, the highest we have ever seen. However, from October/2018 to mid March/2019, the price has dropped $8,000. Bringing the vehicle cost to $47,000, the lowest we have seen up to then.
